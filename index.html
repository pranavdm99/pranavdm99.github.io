Systems Deep Dive

Robotics & Autonomy

Language-Conditioned Robotic Control

Balance task success with energy efficiency and motion smoothness in unpredictable environments.

The Context

Language-conditioned RL (SAC + Sentence-BERT) on MuJoCo for semantic modulation of tasks.

The Outcome

30% reduction in energy, 40% reduction in jerk, and 8x training throughput optimization.

The Pivot: From Success-Only to Energy-Aware

Initial models achieved task success but with excessive jitter and high current draw. Pivoted to a multi-objective reward function incorporating joint torque penalties, mirroring real-world actuator constraints.

Aerial Robotics

CFD-Informed UAV Navigation

Optimize global trajectories for fixed-wing UAVs operating in turbulent, wind-heavy environments.

The Context

Hybrid Bezier-A* global planner (ROS 2) integrating CFD-generated wind-field data for energy-aware routing.

The Proof

Achieved 15% reduction in flight time and 12% improvement in energy efficiency across simulated cross-wind scenarios.

The Action: Solving the Non-Holonomic Constraint

Initial A* paths were too sharp for fixed-wing dynamics. Pivoted to a curvature-constrained Bezier smoothing layer that respects aerodynamic limits while maintaining CFD optimality.

Embedded Systems

BMS & Fleet Telemetry Ecosystem

Deliver high-reliability battery monitoring and remote fleet management for 5000+ deployed units.

The Context

Mission-critical BMS firmware (Cortex-M23) requiring 24/7 reliability and real-time SoC estimation.

The Proof

Increased fleet reliability from 85% to 97.7% through structured root-cause analysis and OTA delta-updates.

Depth: OTA Failure Mode Analysis

Early OTA deployments suffered from intermittent data corruption. Implemented a robust rollback mechanism and persistent flash buffers to ensure zero bricking across bandwidth-constrained deployments.

Edge AI & Signal Processing

TinyML Gait Prediction

Predict lower-limb gait trajectories on-device to minimize latency for active prosthetics control.

The Context

Sub-10ms inference pipeline on Arduino Nano 33 BLE (Cortex-M4) using TensorFlow Lite Micro.

The Outcome

Successfully predicted heel-strike events with 94% accuracy while maintaining a 5mA power envelope.

The Lesson: Quantization vs. Resolution

Full 32-bit models were too slow for real-time control. I optimized the model via post-training 8-bit quantization; the challenge was balancing the loss in precision with the critical need for deterministic sub-10ms timing.
