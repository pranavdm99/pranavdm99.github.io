Reinforcement Learning

Energy-Aware Language-Conditioned Manipulation

Controllable energy-success tradeoffs in robotic manipulation via natural language conditioning.

The Context

Implementing the ECO (Energy-Constrained Optimization) framework on a Franka Panda to map semantic commands like "carefully" to hard physical energy budgets.

The Outcome

Achieved 32% energy savings for "efficient" tasks and reduced mechanical jerk by 80%—improving hardware longevity without retraining.

The Pivot: Physics-Grounded Success

Initial agents prioritized success at any cost, frequently "cheating" by slamming the arm into doors. I grounded the reward in contact sensors, mandating intentful handle-grasping over raw joint completion.

Multi-Agent RL

MAS Drone Coordination (MAPPO vs IPPO)

Advanced coordination tasks (multi-hover, multi-spiral) inspired by NASA's Starling project using Pybullet.

The Insight

Comparative analysis of Multi-Agent PPO (MAPPO) and Independent PPO (IPPO) for coupled aerial maneuvers.

The Proof

MAPPO reduced Spiral RMSE to 0.25m and maintained collision rates at 2.1% in scenarios where independent agents (IPPO) diverged.

The Lesson: Cooperation and Communication

While IPPO suffices for decoupled tasks, coupled behaviors require centralized training with decentralized execution to mitigate the "non-stationary" environment problem inherent in multi-agent systems.

Perception & State Estimation

Robust Kalman Filter for Object Tracking

High-fidelity pose estimation of moving targets (AprilTags) in the presence of extreme sensor noise and outliers.

The Context

Adaptation of the EM-based Robust Kalman Filter (Scaal et al.) for hardware deployment on Raspberry Pi with ROS 2.

The Proof

Reduced Normalized Root Mean Square Error (NRMSE) from 0.094 to 0.019; significantly outperformed standard KF in outlier-heavy scenarios.

The Action: Online Learning

Implemented incremental Expected-Maximization (EM) for online learning of noise parameters, allowing the filter to adapt to changing lighting and sensor quality in real-time.
