Path Planning

CFD-Informed Robust Drone Navigation

Safe autonomous navigation of multi-rotor Crazyflie fleets in unpredictable turbulent environments.

The Context

Integration of CFD-aware cost maps into a hybrid Beziér–A* global planner to account for wind-shear in cluttered spaces.

The Proof

43% reduction in maximum deviation from path during high-wind bursts by routing through wind-shielded zones.

Technical Depth: Cost-Map Robustness

The novelty lay in transforming raw CFD vector fields into a scalar cost-penalty that penalizes high-turbulence regions, ensuring global paths favor aerodynamic stability over shortest-distance.

Multi-Agent RL

MAS Drone Coordination (MAPPO vs IPPO)

Advanced coordination tasks (multi-hover, multi-spiral) inspired by NASA's Starling project using Pybullet.

The Insight

Comparative analysis of Multi-Agent PPO (MAPPO) and Independent PPO (IPPO) for coupled aerial maneuvers.

The Proof

MAPPO reduced Spiral RMSE to 0.25m and maintained collision rates at 2.1% in scenarios where independent agents (IPPO) diverged.

The Lesson: Cooperation and Communication

While IPPO suffices for decoupled tasks, coupled behaviors require centralized training with decentralized execution to mitigate the "non-stationary" environment problem inherent in multi-agent systems.
